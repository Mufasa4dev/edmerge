Hi!, how can we help ?

Frequently asked question

How do i find available scholarship ?

To access scholarships, create an account and log in to your dashboard. On your dashboard you’ll
find a list of available opportunities for you to access

Will I be notified for available training opportunities ?

If you checked the notifications check-box upon sign-up you’ll get notifications. However, you can change your notifications settings anytime on the settings page.

What should I do to have access to trainings ?

To access new trainings, simply sign up and log in to your dashboard where you’ll find available opportunities.

Do I need to subscribe before getting notifications ?

Yes, you have to. However, subscription is free

What do I need to register for a scholarship ?

To register for a scholarship you’ll need the following; academic record (result transcript), National Identity Number (NIN) and application letter.

How do I create an account ?

To create an account simply click signup, fill the required details and verify your information.

How do I change my profile picture ?

To change profile picture, go to ‘settings’ on your dashboard and click on ‘profile picture’

How do I get notifications ?

Notifications will be sent to your email

What do I do when I get selected for a scholarship ?

When selected for a scholarship, the process may defer. You’ll be informed on what to do as peculiar to the scholarship granted to you.

How do I upload my result ?

 To upload result, go to your dashboard and edit the educational background.
